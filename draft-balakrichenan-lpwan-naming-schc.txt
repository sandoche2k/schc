                            Naming for SCHC
                draft-balakrichenan-lpwan-naming-schc-00

Abstract

   This document describes how a DNS label could be send using SCHC SCHC
   for DNS.

1.  Introduction

   [RFC8376]  states that the goal of the LPWAN WG is to, where
   necessary, adapt IETF-defined protocols, addressing schemes, and
   naming conventions to this particular constrained environment.  This
   draft is intended for the naming part.
